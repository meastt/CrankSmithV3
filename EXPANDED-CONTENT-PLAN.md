## 🔍 What the 2026 Gravel Scene is Actually Talking About

**Big tire trend:** 45mm is the new minimum for general gravel. 50mm for aggressive riders/debating: Wider tires feel "ponderous on pavement and squirmy in corners on hard-pack"
- **Racing:** Unbound Gravel (May 28-31, Emporia KS) - world's most iconic, full. Mid South (March 14, Stillwater OK) - new pro/am split format. Life Time Grand Prix - $590K purse, 44 athletes
- **Gearing:** 1x dominates. 2x dying. Mid South pros running: 34×11-42, 42×10-46, 42×9-45, 46×10-46. Campagnolo Ekar 13-speed exists. Anticipated: 13-speed mechanical Shimano MTB drivetrains in 2026
- **Suspension:** Short-travel forks (30-40mm) growing acceptance. RockShox Rudy 30mm gold standard. Elite racers pairing narrow tires + suspension instead of wide tires + rigid
- **Wheel standards:** ETRTO/ISO updated for hookless. 25mm/28mm/30mm internal width debate. Tire inserts becoming standard equipment. Real-time tire pressure sensors emerging as significant tech
- **Groupsets:** Electronic shifting increasingly popular. SRAM AXS premium, SRAM Apex XPLR AXS (budget e-shifting entry), Shimano CUES budget
- **2026 prediction:** "The year of the affordable gravel bike" - Bikeradar. T47 BB becoming universal. UDH required for SRAM Transmission derailleurs
