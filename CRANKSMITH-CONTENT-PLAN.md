## Research Sources (2026 Real Conversations)
- **Gravel racing:** Unbound Gravel (May 28-31), Mid South (March 14), Life Time Grand Prix ($590K purse), SBT GRVL, Rule of Three
- **Tire trends:** 45mm is the new minimum standard. 50mm for aggressive riders. Some frames clearing 57mm (2.25" MTB). Debate: wider tires feel ponderous on pavement and squirmy in corners.
- **Gearing:** 1x dominates. 2x is dying. SRAM AXS electronic for premium, Shimano CUES for budget. Mid South pros running 34×11-42, 42×10-46, 42×9-45 combos.
- **Suspension:** Growing acceptance for short-travel forks (30-40mm). RockShox Rudy 30mm is the gold standard. Elite racers pairing narrow tires with suspension instead of wide tires + rigid.
- **Wheel standards:** ETRTO/ISO updated standards for hookless rims. 25mm/28mm internal width debate. Tire inserts becoming standard equipment.
- **Real-time tire pressure sensors:** Emerging as significant tech addition for gravel in 2026 — riders want data not guesses.
- **Aero gains:** Deep-section rims and narrower tires significant aero improvements for gravel. Position matters more than everything else.
- **2026 predictions:** Bikeradar calls it "the year of the affordable gravel bike." T47 bottom brackets becoming universal. UDH required for SRAM Transmission.
